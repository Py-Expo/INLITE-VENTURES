Welcome

Welcome to Companion Care! We're thrilled to have you on board. This app is designed for nurse to develop their knowledge through microlearning. Get ready for the exciting journey. We're here to make your experience amazing, so dive in and explore!

Vision

1.Always Available: Learners can access education whenever and wherever they want.
2.Empowerment: The app equips users with the tools and knowledge to succeed.
3.Full Potential: Companion care strives to help each learner reach their individual goals.
4.Personalized Learning: Tailored learning paths based on individual progress.
5.Offline Access: Downloading videos and exercises for studying without an internet connection.

About us

COMPANON CARE is mainly built for microlearning of complex topics. It is built for clinical staffs. Through this app a clinical staff can grow their knowledge while working. This app includes quizes, scenario-based training can make learning more engaging and interactive.
