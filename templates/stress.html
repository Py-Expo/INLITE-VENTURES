STRESS MANAGEMENT

Stress management is all about equipping yourself with tools and techniques to handlel life's inevitable challenges in a healthy way. It's not about eliminating stress altogether, but rather learning how to manage it effectively so it doesn't control you.

CHAKRAASANAM
1.Lie down on your back 2.Position your arms 3.Engage your core 4.Arch your back 5.Hold and breathe 6.Come down slowly
Meditation
1.Settle into your position 2.Focus on your breath 3.Acknowledge wandering thoughts 4.Maintain a gentle focus 5.Ending the meditation
Balasanam
1.Begin on your hands and knees 2.Sit back on your heels 3.Fold forward 4.Position your arms 5.Relax your head 6.Breathe deeply
Surya namaskar
1.Breathe deeply and rhythmically 2.Focus on coordinating your breath 3.Move slowly and with control 4.Listen to your body
YOGAMUTRA
1.Find a comfortable seated position 2.Make the hand gesture 3.Hold the mudra for a comfortable amount of time 4.Relax your hands and return to a comfortable position
